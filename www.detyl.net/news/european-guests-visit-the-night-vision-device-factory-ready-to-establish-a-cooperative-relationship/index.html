European guests visit the night vision device factory, ready to establish a cooperative relationship

On July 4th, our night vision device factory was visited by two guests from Europe, who had a detailed understanding of our products and expressed their interest in establishing a cooperative relationship. These two guests are the CEO and purchasing manager of a famous European military night vision sales company, the purpose of this visit is to investigate our production scale and product quality, and explore whether we can establish a partnership.

During the visit to the factory, we showed the guests a variety of models of night vision products, including quadruple vision nvg18, binocular pvs31, dtnvg33, binocular monocular pvs7, monocular pvs14, as well as two-flip bracket and holographic and other product samples. The two guests carefully observed, asked questions and exchanged their experiences and opinions in this field with us. Among them, the quadruple vision this product attracted their special attention, that this night vision device is very excellent, and have considered buying samples for testing.

At noon, we took the two guests to experience the local specialty restaurant, and talked about the development trend of night vision industry and Chinese specialty culture, they were very fond of Chinese food and culture, and thought China is a very warm and energetic country. At the same time, we also had the opportunity to introduce our company’s development history and future plans to them, for the company’s strength and technical means, both guests expressed a strong interest.

After visiting the company, we led the two guests to the new plant to visit, showing them the latest production equipment and technological innovation, the two guests were full of confidence in our strength and future development. Before the end of the visit, the two guests firmly expressed that they were very satisfied with our products and production process, and at the same time, they made clear their attitude and demand for cooperation, and showed that they were full of anticipation for possible cooperation in the future.

This visit not only deepened our mutual understanding, but also the possibility of cooperation. We believe that with deeper cooperation in the future, our production scale and technology will be further improved, so that our night vision products can better meet the needs and expectations of the international market.
