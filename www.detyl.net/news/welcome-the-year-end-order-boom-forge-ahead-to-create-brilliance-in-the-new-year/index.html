Welcome the year-end order boom, forge ahead to create brilliance in the new year.

Recently, our company welcomed the year-end order boom. All the staff of the production department worked at full capacity and worked overtime to rush orders, just so that customers can receive products faster.

With the growth of market demand at the end of the year, the company’s products have explosively increased in orders due to their excellent quality and good reputation. The production workshop is busy, all production lines are running at full capacity, and workers are racing against time to ensure efficient output of products.

This order boom is not only an affirmation of the company’s efforts in the past year, but also a good start for the new year. The company’s management is optimistic that the turnover in the new year is expected to reach a new high. The person in charge of the company said: “Thank you very much for the trust and support of our customers for our products. In the future, we will seize the opportunity to expand the scale of production lines, further improve product quality, optimize production processes, and speed up production efficiency.”

It is reported that the company has formulated detailed expansion plans for popular products such as PGNVG, PVS31, and PVS7. In order to give back to our customers, we now open the advance reservation channel, and you can enjoy discounts by booking.

On the occasion of the upcoming Spring Festival, all employees of Shenzhen Detyl Co., Ltd. extend their sincere New Year greetings to all walks of life, wishing everyone a happy New Year and a fruitful harvest in the new year.
