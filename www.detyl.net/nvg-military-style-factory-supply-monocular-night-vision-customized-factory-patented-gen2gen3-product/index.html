Mil-Std Gen2+/Gen3 NVM PVS14

Model: DTS-14

Short Description:

The DTS-14 night vision is a new product developed based on the latest optoelectronics technology
with a field of view of 50 degrees.The magnification can be
changed by replacing the objective lens. The night vision device has a built-in infrared Illuminator and
an auto- gain system.

Send email to us

Product Detail

Product Tags

 

Main Features

1. IP65 waterproof standard(IP67 optional);
2. Build in IR indicator and low battery indicator;
3. Optical distortion less than 0.5%;
4. Mil-spec multi-coated optics;
5. 30mm diameter eyepeice lens;
6. Longer eye relief 25-40mm with full FOV;
7. Normal use CR123A battery, also offer AA battery version;
8. AUTO shut OFF/ON when flip up/down, save power when not use;
9. Offer 3X and 5X magnifier lens;
10. Eyepiece lens and objective lens coated with demist coating;
11. Offer sacrificial windows(optional);
12. Strong light auto shut off;
